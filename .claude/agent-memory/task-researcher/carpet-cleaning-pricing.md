# Carpet Cleaning Pricing Research Notes
Researched: 2026-03-03
Source: Claude trained knowledge (through Aug 2025); WebSearch/WebFetch unavailable

## Key Pricing Data Points

### Base Rates (Residential, Steam/HWE)
- Per room: $25–$75 (average ~$45)
- Per sq ft: $0.10–$0.25
- Minimum charge: $89–$100
- Whole home (3-bed): $150–$250 typical

### Room Size Multipliers (vs standard 150-250 sq ft room)
- Small (<150 sq ft): 0.85x
- Medium (150-250 sq ft): 1.0x
- Large (250-350 sq ft): 1.4x
- XL (350+ sq ft): 1.8x

### Soil Level Multipliers
- Light: 1.0x
- Moderate: 1.2x
- Heavy: 1.4x
- Severe: 1.6x

### Carpet Type Multipliers
- Standard synthetic: 1.0x
- Berber/loop: 1.15x
- Frieze/shag: 1.20x
- Wool: 1.35x

### Key Add-Ons (per room unless noted)
- Pet stain (topical): $25–$40
- Pet odor enzymatic: $35–$55
- Scotchgard protector: $20–$30
- Deodorizing: $12–$20
- Furniture moving: $20–$30
- Spot treatment (per stain): $15–$25
- Stairs (per step): $2–$5
- Stairs (per flight): $35–$75
- Hallway: $15–$40
- Upholstery sofa: $80–$175

### Volume Discounts (on room subtotal only)
- 5+ rooms: 10% off
- 8+ rooms: 15% off

## Quote Data Collection Fields (Priority Order)
1. Residential vs commercial
2. Number of rooms
3. Room sizes (S/M/L/XL)
4. Stairs? (steps or flights)
5. Hallways/closets?
6. Carpet type
7. Cleaning method preference
8. Soil level
9. Pet issues (none / hair only / some accidents / severe urine)
10. Specific stains?
11. Add-on interest (Scotchgard, deodorizing, furniture moving, upholstery)

## Escalation Triggers (route to human, not firm quote)
- Sub-floor pet urine damage reported
- Mold or mildew mentioned
- Water damage mentioned
- Commercial jobs >1,000 sq ft
